May Myat Noe

Burmese Model

May Myat Noe(That Htet Aung) was born on October 13, 1998. She is a Burmese fashion model and beauty pageant title holder.
She is best known for winning Miss Asia Pacific World in 2014, as well as being a contestant in the fourth season of Asia's Next Top Model in 2016.
